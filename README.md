# iCal Pro for Obsidian

**Bridge the gap between AI-driven task management and your global calendar.**

iCal Pro is a professional-grade synchronization engine that turns your Obsidian tasks into live, synced calendar events.

## Core Functions
- **Automated Scheduling**: Automatically parses tasks with dates (supports standard dates and emojis like `📅`, `🛫`, `⏳`) and generates iCalendar events.
- **Rich Context Capture**: Intelligently extracts indented notes, lists, or blockquotes listed *under* your tasks and includes them in the calendar description.
- **Incremental Indexing**: Powered by a memory-resident `TaskIndex` that only re-parses modified files. Zero performance impact even on massive vaults.
- **Timezone Intelligence**: Uses "Floating Time" combined with `X-WR-TIMEZONE` headers to keep your schedule accurate regardless of your location.
- **Deep Linking**: Every event includes a direct `obsidian://` link. Open your specific note from any mobile or desktop calendar app in one click.

## Key Needs Solved
1. **AI Agent Workflows**: Seamlessly sync plans generated by AI agents in your daily notes to your mobile devices.
2. **Universal Accessibility**: Access your local Obsidian tasks on Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook.
3. **Detailed Schedules**: View high-density information (flight numbers, meeting agendas) directly inside your calendar events.

## Installation (via BRAT)
Until the plugin is available on the official community store, use **BRAT** for easy installation and updates:
1. Install and enable the [BRAT](https://github.com/TfTHacker/obsidian42-brat) plugin.
2. Go to BRAT settings and click **"Add Beta plugin"**.
3. Enter the repository URL: `liuh886/obsidian-ical-plugin-pro`
4. Click **"Add Plugin"** and then enable **iCal Pro** in your community plugins list.

## Quick Setup
1. **GitHub Sync**: Create a [Personal Access Token](https://github.com/settings/tokens?type=beta) with `Gist` scope and a new [Gist](https://gist.github.com/).
2. **Configure**: Enter your GitHub Username, Gist ID, and Token in the iCal Pro settings pane.
3. **Verify**: Use the **"Test GitHub Sync"** button to confirm connectivity.
4. **Subscribe**: Copy your unique **Subscription URL** and add it to your calendar app (e.g., "Add by URL").
